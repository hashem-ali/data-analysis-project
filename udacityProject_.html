Medical Appointment Dataset¶

In this we will be analyzing this dataset to answer a some qustions about people who miss thier oppinmemnt:¶

  • Is there a relationship between age and not showing up
  • Do alcholic people tend to miss their doctor appointments
  • Do uneducated people tend to miss doctor appointments
  • Is there a relationship between gender type and missing an appointment
  • Is sending SMS help to remind Patients and keep their appointments

Conclusions¶

    To sum up, from the above graohs we can conclude the following:
  • Females tend to not show for their appointments.
  • we notice that uneducated people are more likely to not show up.
  • There is no relationship between alcoholic people and not showing up.
  • There is no relationship between receiving SMS and not showing up.
  • We noticed that people tend to miss their appointments on Tuesday and Wednesday.
  • We notice that the older patient are more patient will show up.
